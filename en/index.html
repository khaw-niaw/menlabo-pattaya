Our Story

In Awaza, Osaka,
there was a small ramen shop.

Chicken broth, no MSG.
A bowl built fresh every day,
with nothing cut short.
That obsession crossed the sea
and landed here in Pattaya.

Long-time residents.
Travelers passing through.
People who fell for Thailand and stayed.
We're here to make a place where,
at the end of the day, you can say:
"Now that was a good night."

The "LABO" in MENLABO means laboratory.
No two days taste the same.

A diner by day. A tavern from three.
