2. Information We Collect

The Site does not offer features such as account registration that require you to enter your own information. However, when you browse the Site, we may automatically collect the following information:

In addition, if you contact us by email, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your inquiry.

3. Purpose of Use

We use the collected information only within the scope of the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site

To understand how the Site is used, we use “Google Analytics,” provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect your browsing data; this data is collected anonymously and does not identify individuals.

You can stop this data collection by disabling cookies in your browser settings or by using the opt-out add-on provided by Google. For how Google handles data, please see the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies

The Site may use cookies to improve usability and for analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but in that case some features of the Site may not work correctly.

Except as required by law, we do not provide collected personal data to third parties without first obtaining your consent. However, information may be sent to the analytics and map service providers described above, to the extent necessary to provide those services.

8. Management of Personal Data

We manage the personal data we collect appropriately and strive to prevent its leakage, loss, or damage.
