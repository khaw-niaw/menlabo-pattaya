Getting here from key spots

  • From Walking Street: ~1.6 km, 5–15 min by car (busy at night)
  • From Central Pattaya Road: ~10 min on foot from the Soi Buakhao entrance
  • From Terminal 21 Pattaya: ~3.3 km, 8–20 min by car (depending on traffic)

* Pattaya traffic varies by time and day. For an accurate current travel time, check the map above.

Getting around

  • Taxi: just say “Soi Buakhao, Tree Town”
  • Songthaew (shared): around ฿15. Name a destination and it may become a private hire at ฿100–200
  • Motorbike taxi: easy for short hops
  • On foot: inside Tree Town, look for the lantern
